🎯 Final Outcome

By the end of this bootcamp, you will be able to:

Design a production-ready AI architecture.
Build an AI agent without relying on frameworks.
Understand how frameworks such as LangGraph and CrewAI work internally.
Add memory, planning, tool calling, RAG, and multi-agent collaboration to your own applications.
Build REST APIs around your agent.
Test, containerize, and prepare the system for deployment.
Showcase a professional GitHub project during interviews.
